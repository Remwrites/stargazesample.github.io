Laser Hair Removal Body

We offer the excellent 3D Trilogyice machine, a revolutionary laser equipment that delivers a pleasant and effective laser hair removal treatment for most skin types and locations of the body. With a good quality at a cheap price and no sacrifice on technology. When compared to single wavelength options, 3D Trilogyice combines the proven benefits of three wavelengths, allowing clinics to achieve maximum results and adaptability. These three wavelengths transmit energy downward, reaching various levels of dermis tissue. The 3D Trilogyice uses volumetric heating of the dermal tissue to change the function of the hair. Each laser pulse is administered at a modest but fast fluence to collectively heat the dermal tissue and thermally damage the hair follicle. The melanin in the hair helps to transfer heat, raising the follicle temperature above the skin tissue temperature. Each laser handpiece pulse is shorter in duration than the thermal relaxation period of the hair follicle. This permits energy to accumulate within the follicle, resulting in irreparable heat stress. The sensitive skin tissue, on the other hand, remains untouched. This damage significantly inhibits the function of the biological factors involved in hair re-growth.

Laser Hair Removal Face

Our Laser Hair Removal therapy is safe to use on any part of the body and targets different skin types while ensuring the best possible outcomes. It was created with sensitive skin in mind. Our lasers all include a unique dynamic cooling system that helps to protect, calm, and soothe the skin. With each treatment, the hair becomes finer and the growth rate slows. Ingrown hairs, rashes, and stubble are considerably minimised when compared to more traditional hair removal treatments such as waxing, threading, shaving, or utilising hair removal lotions.

How Does This Work?

Lasers deliver a high-intensity laser pulse into the hair follicles. That energy is converted to heat, which is absorbed by the hair, causing chromophores (called melanin or pigment) to be damaged and preventing future hair development. It’s a painless, efficient approach to remove hair without the discomfort or bother of conventional treatments such as waxing. After your initial set of treatments, you just need to maintain your smooth, hairless skin 1 to 2 times each year. Each session reduces hair growth by roughly 20%, which is why several treatments are required. In most situations, our clients get the desired effects within 6 to 12 sessions.

When will i see results?

Hair starts falling out around 2 weeks following your treatment, but it’s critical to finish the required number of sessions for best results. Laser hair removal typically needs 6-10 sessions, depending on hair growth, hair type and colour, and removal region. Appointments for the face should be 4 weeks apart, while appointments for the body should be 6 weeks apart.

Is it a permanent removal?

While laser hair removal is not a permanent cure, it does greatly inhibit future hair growth in treated regions. Hair will regrow sparse, thinner, and perhaps lighter in colour. For best results, we recommend maintenance treatments once or twice a year, depending on how your hair responds.

What's the downtime?

While laser hair removal has no downtime, you may notice some redness afterward. We recommend that you avoid the sun for two weeks before and one week after treatment.
