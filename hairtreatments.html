Hair Treatments

We provide a selection of hair treatments to ensure you receive the best care and results tailored to your individual needs.

Hair Filler

Hair filler aids in the prevention of hair loss, the restoration of hair, and the maintenance of the scalp. Lower scalp blood circulation and a lack of nutrients feeding hair follicle growth will have an impact on hair health, hair loss, and look. When your hair is thin and there is only one hair within one hair follicle, as compared to 2-3 hairs in one hair follicle in patients with good skin and nourished hair, you can use Hair Filler. This aids in the prevention of hair loss by increasing blood circulation to the scalp and stimulating hair growth. Using a Hair Filler may be beneficial to your condition, but hair-related tests should be performed to determine the cause of your hair loss or baldness.

PRP Hair

PRP therapy works on the basis that the body’s own natural healing powers may slow and even reverse the ageing process. The technique is a clinically proven medical procedure that uses the science of stem cells, and the healing factors in your own blood, as a safe and effective alternative to other treatments. With just one consultation you’ll be able to start your rejuvenation process. When applying PRP to the hair, it can regenerate and increase the production of new growth to the scalp and beard.

Mesotherapy

Facial Mesotherapy is a novel procedure for rejuvenating and improving the health and look of the skin. The therapy infuses the lowest layer of your skin with a customised combination of nutrients such as hyaluronic acid, vitamins, minerals, and FDA-approved medications. Facial Mesotherapy is one of the most effective skin treatments for reversing apparent indicators of ageing, including pollution, sun exposure, lifestyle factors, and stress. Through an innovative power injector into the lowest layer of your skin, the Facial Mesotherapy treatment offers a personalised and complex combination of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, amino acids, and hyaluronic acid.
