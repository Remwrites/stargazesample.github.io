RF Microneedling

3D DERMAFORCE RADIO FREQUENCY

How does this work?

The cosmetic technique radiofrequency microneedling rejuvenates face skin using radiofrequency radiation and small needles. The procedure involves carefully delivering radio frequency radiation through insulated needles to the dermis, heating the collagen strands to a temperature between 55 and 65 degrees Celsius. Collagen and elastin are produced in these places as a result of this stimulating the body’s natural healing process. Damage encourages the creation of fresh, healthy skin, which can help with common skin problems like wrinkles and acne scars.

What results can you expect?

Due to the thickening of the dermal tissue brought on by collagen formation, your skin will appear plump. Lines, wrinkles, and crepey skin will improve as a result. The skin tone will balance out and pigmentation areas will become better. enhances the texture of the skin. In the course of therapy, stretch marks and loose skin can also be addressed.

What areas can be treated?

Both the face and the body may be used safely. It will improve slack skin, fine lines and wrinkles, pigmented skin from sun damage, stretch marks, and skin that is pitted.

Is there any downtime?

Your skin may become pink and swell somewhat; these symptoms might persist up to three days. We advise you to apply SPF and moisturising creams at home every day if your skin feels a little bit dry.

How does it feel?

In order to precisely reach the dermis during therapy, radio frequency radiation is administered using insulated, gold-plated needles. When applied, there is a mild pressure and warmth in the skin. After treatment, your skin may be somewhat sensitive and may feel similar to how it does after being in the sun for a while.

How much will the treatment cost?

Prices will vary depending on the size of the area so it can range from £200 – 350.

What Can RF Microneedling Do?

The skin that regenerates after each treatment leaves the skin feeling both smoother and thicker. This will target:

  • Wrinkles
  • Fine lines
  • Cellulite
  • Acne Scars
  • Sagging Skin
  • Stretch Marks
  • Hyperhidrosis
  • Injury/Surgery Scars
  • Hyperpigmentation
