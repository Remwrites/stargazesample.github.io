PRP Face Treatment

PRP therapy works on the basis that the body’s own natural healing powers may slow and even reverse the ageing process. The technique is a clinically proven medical procedure that uses the science of stem cells, and the healing factors in your own blood, as a safe and effective alternative to a surgical face-lift procedure. With just one consultation you’ll be able to start your rejuvenation process. When applying PRP to the skin, it can regenerate and rejuvenate your skin which causes skin problems such as acne, acne scarring, scarring, wrinkles, pigmentation, puffy eyes, baggy eyes and more.

Micro Needling

Micro needling is a treatment that uses tiny needles to make minute punctures in the skin. These little contact points trigger the body’s wound healing response and skin cell regeneration. The creation of collagen and elastin is promoted while your skin recovers, giving in an almost immediate plumping effect. It can also help with scars, dark spots, sun damage, and ageing of the skin.

Dermaplanning

Dermaplaning is an extremely efficient exfoliating technique. Dermaplaning is a medical-grade exfoliation that involves the controlled removal of the skin’s outermost layer using a tiny scalpel. The scalpel is carefully used to remove dead skin cells and vellus hair from the skin. The procedure aims to make the skin on the face seem smooth, youthful, and radiant. Dermaplaning, in addition to smoothing skin and removing “peach fuzz,” proponents say it can help with acne scars, dry skin, sun-damaged skin, and small wrinkles.

Mesotherapy

Facial Mesotherapy is a novel procedure for rejuvenating and improving the health and look of the skin. The therapy infuses the lowest layer of your skin with a customised combination of nutrients such as hyaluronic acid, vitamins, minerals, and FDA-approved medications. Facial Mesotherapy is one of the most effective skin treatments for reversing apparent indicators of ageing, including pollution, sun exposure, lifestyle factors, and stress. Through an innovative power injector into the lowest layer of your skin, the Facial Mesotherapy treatment offers a personalised and complex combination of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, amino acids, and hyaluronic acid.
