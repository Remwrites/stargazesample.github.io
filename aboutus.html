What is Stargaze Beauty?

Stargaze Aesthetic Clinic was founded with a mission to provide top-notch aesthetic treatments and holistic skincare solutions. Our clinic is dedicated to enhancing the health and well-being of our clients through personalized and effective treatments. We are passionate about offering exceptional results and ensuring that every client feels confident and revitalized.

Meet Our CEO

Stephanie Grant - Founder and CEO

Stephanie Grant is the visionary behind Stargaze Aesthetic Clinic. With over 10 years of experience as a Registered General Nurse, Stephanie has a rich background in advanced nursing care within acute medical settings. Her expertise includes managing patients with complex conditions, including pre and post-operative care for invasive diagnostic and therapeutic procedures.

A Journey to Aesthetic Excellence

Stephanie’s personal journey began with her struggle against chronic acne, which led her to explore alternative treatments. Her search for effective solutions introduced her to the world of aesthetic beauty. Through diligent research and experimentation, Stephanie discovered the Vampire Facial (PRP), a treatment that not only cured her acne but also transformed her life.

Advancing Holistic Skincare

Inspired by her own results, Stephanie delved deeper into holistic skin therapies. She studied various treatments, including PRP for hair restoration, Microneedling, and Platelet Rich Plasma techniques. Her dedication to these advanced therapies has enabled her to help thousands of individuals regain their confidence with customized skin treatments.

A Renowned Expert

Today, Stephanie is recognized as a leading skin expert in the Aesthetics & Beauty Industry. Clients from across the country seek her expertise at Stargaze Beauty, drawn by her reputation for exceptional care and results. Stephanie and her team are committed to providing professional and ethical services, adhering to the NMC code of conduct, and delivering outstanding aesthetic outcomes and aftercare.

Our Mission

Being in Aesthetic Beauty for a few years, we’ve developed new techniques and skills in the way we provide our treatments. We are dedicated and passionate when it comes to delivering results and enhancing our client’s beauty and confidence. Our aim is to offer treatments that don’t require the use of harsh chemicals, our PRP treatments mainly focus on getting the plasma from your own blood. This plasma provides miracles in the naturalist and safest way possible, we see this as a pathway to a new type of Aesthetic Beauty that’s 100% natural and works on all types of skin for all ethnic groups.
