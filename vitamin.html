Vitamin Therapy

If you have vitamin deficiencies, digestive troubles, chronic diseases, exercise frequently, or are always on the go, an IV drip is great. Vitamins, minerals, and amino acids are essential for your physical and mental well-being. They aid in the functions of your body, keeping your organs and even your DNA healthy and running regularly.

Vitamn B12 Injection

Vitamin B12 is a vitamin that aids in the health of your neurons and blood cells. Vitamin B12 is not produced by our bodies; instead, it is taken through foods such as meat, fish, milk, and eggs. You may be deficient if you are vegan, do not consume enough B12-rich foods, or have other health issues that prevent you from adequately absorbing it, such as pernicious anaemia.

Vitamin D Injection

Vitamin D is a fat-soluble vitamin that increases calcium and phosphate absorption in the gut. Vitamin D is essential for bone, tooth, and muscle health. This essential nutrient is required for many of the body’s main activities and helps the body absorb other minerals. Vitamin D is critical to your general health and well-being.
